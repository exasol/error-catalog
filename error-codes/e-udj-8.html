E-UDJ-8

Message

Could not find jprofiler archive or open on path {{agent path}}).

Mitigation

Please download the JProfiler for Linux without JRE from the JProfiler website and specify the commandline option -DjProfilerAgent=<PATH TO jprofiler.tar.gz> or save it as ~/jprofiler.tar.gz.
